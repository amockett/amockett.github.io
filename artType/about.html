About Andrew

Andrew's work has been extensively exhibited in touring exhibitions in public galleries across the UK, USA and South East Asia. Andrew is currently represented by the Rebecca Hossack Gallery, in Conway Street, London and Mott Street, New York.

He is currently living in Italy.
